Explore the remnants of a ruined kingdom

StarVeil is a Hollow Knight-like metroidvania where you dash through enemies in order to attack them. Due to this mechanic, the combat requires precise timing and a bit of skill. The game is also inspired by Celeste having an 8-directional dash and even including wave dashing and other mechanics from celeste.

Explore different Environments

Venture through a variety of unique locations, each with its own atmosphere, challenges, and secrets to uncover. From rocky mountains to deep underwater cities, every environment offers new sights, foes and obstacles to overcome.
